About Me

Hi there! I'm Sarah, a passionate pet lover with over 5 years of professional pet sitting experience. I understand that your furry family members deserve the best care while you're away, and I'm here to provide exactly that.

I offer personalized care for each pet, maintaining their routine and providing lots of love and attention. Whether it's daily walks, feeding schedules, or just companionship, I treat every pet as if they were my own.

I'm available for both short-term and long-term sitting arrangements, and I always provide regular updates with photos so you can have peace of mind while you're away.
